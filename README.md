# introduction.github


This space is used by Zehua Xu’s group at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), focusing on experimental high energy physics. The group is currently involved in the LHCb experiment at CERN and in preliminary studies for the Super Tau Charm Facility (STCF).

This GitHub organization hosts common resources shared within Zehua Xu’s group. Some LHCb-specific resources are maintained on [CERN GitLab](https://gitlab.cern.ch/ustc-xu-group).
